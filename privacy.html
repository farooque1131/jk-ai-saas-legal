Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our omnichannel AI inbox services. This includes business configuration data and inbound user messages transmitted via integrated APIs.

2. How We Use WhatsApp Data

CRITICAL COMPLIANCE NOTICE: We do not sell, rent, or share WhatsApp user data, phone numbers, or conversation histories with any third-party advertising or marketing agencies. Data obtained via the WhatsApp Business API is used strictly to provide the requested AI automation and human-escalation services to the verified business owner.

3. Data Retention

Message logs and AI interaction data are securely stored to allow human agents to review and take over conversations. Verified business owners can request the deletion of their workspace data at any time.

4. Data Deletion Instructions

Users or business entities wishing to delete their account data or any processed WhatsApp interaction data can submit a formal request via email to contact@jkenterprises.com (or use your personal contact email for now). All associated customer data will be permanently purged from our databases within 30 days of verification.
